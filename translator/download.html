80 Inspirational Quotes for When Your Mood Could Use a Boost

These short positive sayings can work wonders.

By Zee Krstic and Caroline Picard
Apr 29, 2022
D-Keine

Finding motivation and inspiration while you're in the midst of the hectic daily grind can be very hard, let alone if a challenge or hurdle is blocking an optimistic outlook. The stressors behind your career, in your relationships, and in life at large can take a mental toll if you're not careful. It's why taking time to look to other inspiring leaders for motivation can help keep the flame of positivity burning bright in your life, at any age. That's where our list of the very best inspirational quotes comes in! These short, powerful and (often!) funny snippets of speech, written excerpts and celebrated metaphors provide a burst of inspiration to get you through anything coming your way in 2022.

Below, you'll find a list of words of wisdom from beloved figures — powerhouse actors, musicians, authors and more — who have experienced a lot of ups and downs, but still managed to derive some insight through it all. From Michelle Obama to Demi Lovato, C.S Lewis to Franklin Roosevelt, these positive affirmations from your favorite celebrities will uplift you, empower you and get you ready for the next chapter in your life.
